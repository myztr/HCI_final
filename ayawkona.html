Our Services

Haircuts

Consultation:
A consultation with a stylist to discuss your desired haircut, hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. This helps determine the most suitable style for you.

Washing and Conditioning:
Some salons start with washing and conditioning your hair to cleanse it and make it more manageable for cutting.

Cutting:
The actual haircut based on the agreed-upon style. This can involve different cutting techniques like layering, texturizing, or precision cutting.

Styling:
After the haircut, your hair may be styled using techniques like blow-drying, flat ironing, or curling to achieve the desired look.

Finishing Products:
Application of finishing products such as hair gel, mousse, or hairspray to set the style and enhance the overall look.

Beard Trims

Consultation:
Similar to haircuts, a consultation with a barber or stylist is often conducted to understand your preferences and the desired outcome for your beard.

Trimming:
The barber will use specialized tools such as clippers, scissors, or trimmers to trim your beard to the desired length. This includes evening out the length, shaping the edges, and removing any stray or split ends.

Detailing:
Precision detailing around the mustache, sideburns, and neckline to create clean lines and enhance the overall appearance of the beard.

Styling:
If desired, the barber may style the beard using products like beard oil, balm, or wax to add texture, control, and shine.

Finishing Touches:
The service may conclude with hot towel treatments to soothe the skin and moisturize the beard, leaving it looking and feeling fresh.
